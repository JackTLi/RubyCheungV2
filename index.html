I am currently a 4th year student at University of Waterloo. I am studying Global Business and Digital Arts. As a UX designer I am able to design for different platforms, whether it is in a digital form or a physical form. Throughout my portfolio you will see different pieces done in different mediums.

 

I'm specialized in

RESPONSIVE DESIGN

Is creating websites that could be viewed on multiple platforms. This is essential because users will view websites depending on what is convenient for them.

PROTOTYPING

Helps me to identify the elements that will be displayed on the page of the screen. It also allows me to change the position of the elements when doing a usability testing on a product.

INTERACTION DESIGN

This is an important aspect of being a user experience designer because this is how users interact with your product. To produce a good interaction is to create an effective user journey that allows the user to flow through the product easily.

STORYTELLING

Is an important aspect that is necessary in any company's brand. Storytelling allows companies to connect with the audience on an emotional level. This can be achieved through a collection of written content, videos, and pictures.
